# World Notes — Midgaard

## City hub (safe, low-level area)
- **The Bakery** — north of Main Street. Sells danish (7g)/bread (14g)/waybread (71g).
  This is where sessions currently start. Exit: s.
- **Main Street** — center hub. Armory to the south, Bakery to the north,
  Market Square to the east. Exits: n e s w.
- **The Armory** — south of Main Street. Sells armor (leather/bronze gear,
  91g-1217g range). Exit: n.
- **Market Square** — east of Main Street. Roads to Temple Square (n),
  Common Square (s), Main Street (e/w — layout is a bit circular/symmetric).
  Exits: n e s w.
- **Temple Square** — north of Market Square. Clerics' Guild to the west,
  the old Grunting Boar Inn to the east. Exits: n e s w.
- **The Temple Of Midgaard** — north of Temple Square, through the temple
  gate. This is the classic CircleMUD newbie/recall hub — Reading Room to
  the west, donation room to the east, an ATM-like teller machine here.
  Exits: n e s w d (haven't explored past this room yet).
- **Common Square** — south of Market Square. Poor Alley to the west, Dark
  Alley to the east. 3x "beastly fido" mobs tend to be here. ⚠️ **Do not
  fight here** — a Peacekeeper NPC breaks up fights and hit dummy for ~15
  HP in one round, killing dummy from 17 HP (see player.md "Died once").
  Exits: n e s w.
- **The Dark Alley** — east of Common Square. Guild of Thieves to the
  south. Mercenaries and a cityguard idle here (not hostile, didn't
  engage). Continues east to **The Dark Alley At The Levee** (leads to a
  levee to the south, unexplored).
- **The Guild of Magic Users** — south of Main Street's far west end
  (unexplored inside). **The Magic Shop** is just north of that same spot —
  sells scrolls/wands (233g-5832g), nothing affordable at low level and no
  weapons.
- **The City Gate** — west of Main Street's far west end (unexplored, but
  likely another "above your level" zone transition like the South Gate —
  treat with the same caution until scouted).

## Poor Alley / western wall (low-level, quiet)
- **Poor Alley** (eastern end) — Grubby Inn to the south (unexplored),
  connects to Common Square (e) and continues west. A Peacekeeper NPC
  patrols here.
- **Poor Alley** (western stretch) — a beggar NPC here, continues to Wall
  Road (w).
- **Wall Road** (x2 rooms heading north along the western city wall) —
  leads to a bridge south.
- **On The Bridge** — stone bridge over the river, city wall to the west.
- **The Northwest/Southwest End Of The Concourse** — wraps around the SW
  corner of the city wall.

## ⚠️ Danger: south/west Concourse exit
- From "On The Concourse" (the SW-corner room, "leads both north and
  east"), going **west** leaves the city entirely via **The South Gate**,
  onto a path toward the forest of Miden'Nir. Game explicitly warns "This
  zone is above your recommended level" — do not go past the South Gate at
  low level.

## Park / Emerald Avenue district (north side, explored but not mapped in detail)
- Emerald Avenue, Penny Lane, Park Road, The Promenade, and "A Path/Small
  Path In The Park" form a loop north of the Concourse. Ducks and sparrows
  wander here — cosmetic, not hostile. The Road Crossing (with the road
  sign and the big chain leading "up" to Redferne's Flying Citadel) sits
  at the center. Found 37 gold coins at "The End Of Penny Lane."
- Not yet connected on the map to the Temple/Market Square hub — got there
  via Main Street/Armory in an earlier session, and via the Concourse loop
  in this one; haven't found the direct link between the two clusters yet.

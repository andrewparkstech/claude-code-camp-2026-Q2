# World Notes

## Midgaard - The Bakery
- Location: From Temple of Midgaard: d, s (Market Square), w (Main Street - Armory/Bakery), n
- Room desc: small bakery, sweet scent of danish and fine bread. Baker NPC present. Exit: s only.
- Sign on counter: `buy` purchases bread/pastry, `list` shows prices.

### Menu (via `list`)
| # | Item | Cost | Available |
|---|------|------|-----------|
| 1 | A danish pastry | 7 | Unlimited |
| 2 | A bread | 14 | Unlimited |
| 3 | A waybread | 71 | Unlimited |

## Midgaard - Known Layout
- Temple of Midgaard (start room): exits n e s w d
  - d -> Temple Square (Clerics' Guild w, Grunting Boar Inn e, fountain, market square s)
    - s -> Market Square (statue; n=temple square, e/w=main street, s=common square)
      - e -> Main Street: general store (n), Pet Shop (s), continues e
        - e -> Main Street (edge of town): weapon shop (n), Guild of Swordsmen (s), e leaves town
      - w -> Main Street: Armory (s), Bakery (n)
        - n -> The Bakery (dead end, exit s only)
      - s -> Common Square: poor alley (w), dark alley (e), nasty smell to the s
